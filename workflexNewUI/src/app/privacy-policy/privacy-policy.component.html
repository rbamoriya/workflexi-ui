Privacy policy

Updated on August 01, 2018

We at Vigour Contingent Private Limited are committed to respecting the privacy of our users and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information ("Personal Information") you share with us. "Personal Information" means any information that may be used to identify an individual, including, but not limited to, a first and last name, a home or other physical address and an email address or other contact information, whether at work or at home. Personal Information are the information that can be used to identify the person and we do not collect personal information unless you choose to provide it to us. Some of them are Name, Telephone Number, Email Address, Telephone Number, Contact Information.

We do not collect sensitive personal data like financial information such as Bank Account details or Credit Card or Debit Card or other payment details, health conditions, Biometric functions like fingerprints, DNA, Voice & Facial patterns. We do not store information of users like password of your email account.

Confidentiality and Security

We use the information from the website ( www.workflexi.in ) for the following purpose:

  • For providing the services mentioned.
  • To provide a favourable user experience
  • Foster the service we offer to job seekers and employers
  • Continuous improvement of website and its features.

Users are responsible for ensuring their log-in credentials (Username & password) are kept confidential.

If you wish to delete or close your account or account profile information altogether, please contact us at contact@workflexi.in

While we take every precaution to secure your personal information from accidental loss and / or from unauthorized access or use, internet being an open system, we cannot guarantee fully that unauthorized third parties will not be able to defeat those measures or use your personal information for improper purposes. Please ensure that your resume does not contain any sensitive data that you would not want made public. Your profile may also be monitored by your current employer.

Disclosure & sharing information

We disclose user’s profile information to employers who are our trusted partners who work with us under confidentiality agreements. These partners may use job seeker’s personal information given in the website to communicate with the job seeker about the opportunities.

Once the resume is uploaded in the website, it is searchable & accessible to all the employers whom we are partnered with. The job seeker’s resume may be viewed and access by all employers registered with us and at the moment we do not provide the option to a job seeker to block a particular employer.
