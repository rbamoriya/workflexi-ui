What We Do

We are a platform for gigs. Gigs are quick short-term jobs that help you earn money. Our gigs are quick and remunerative. Could vary from 1 day gig to 1 year gig.

We offer peer-to-peer, employer-to-employee or business-to-business gigs. Look for a skill -> Engage with them -> Pay, get your work done.

What makes us unique

We are India’s only platform to offer gigs across different verticals. Gigs could be contractual or freelancing in nature.

We eliminate the concept of a ‘Resume’. Instead we have a 30sec video of a gig worker showcasing his/her skill.

Through a single log in, you can achieve four different objectives:

  • Operate as an individual gig-worker
  • Operate as a company gig-seeker
  • Operate as an individual hirer
  • Operate as a company hirer
